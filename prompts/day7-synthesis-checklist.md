# Day 7 synthesis checklist

Use this checklist yourself. ruta must not write the synthesis for you.

- What is the spec trying to accomplish, in one sentence, in your own words?
- Which propositions are most load-bearing?
- Which contracts matter most between actors?
- Which unresolved gaps still affect implementation risk?
- Which failure modes are predictable from the text?
- Which claims in your synthesis cite the exact passage they rely on?
